Live Fit Meal Plan

An 8-Week Transformation Challenge

Breakfast

  • Week 1
    1. 1. Spinach & Feta Omelette: 2 Eggs, 30g Feta Cheese, 1 Handful fresh Spinach.

    2. 2. Nut Butter & Berry Rice Cakes: 4 Rice / Corn Cakes, 2 Tbsp Peanut / Almond Butter, 100g Mixed Berries.

    3. 3. Protein Smoothie: 1 Scoop Jenna Carmen Shake, 1 Cup Ice, 250ml Plant-based or Fat Free Milk & 1tbsp Peanut butter.

    4. 4. Crackerbread Please: 3-4 Crackerbread, Marmite, Lightly Buttered.

  • Week 2
    1. 1. Tropical Smoothie Bowl: 150g Fat Free yogurt, 80g Pineapple, 80g Blueberries.

    2. 2. Egg Please: 2 Lrg Egg, 2 Tbsp Fat Free Cottage Cheese, Spring Onion, 30g Avocado, 3-4 Rice Cakes.

    3. 3. Egg White Omelette: 3 Egg whites, 80g Chicken (Weigh Cooked), Tomato & Onion, 80g Mushrooms.

    4. 4. Jenna Carmen Flapjacks: 3 Jenna Carmen Flapjacks With Honey & 150g Mixed Berries.

  • Week 3
    1. 1. Jenna Carmen Flapjacks: 3 Jenna Carmen Flapjacks With Honey & 80g Strawberries.
    2. 2. Eggs & Bacon Please: 2 Eggs (Boiled / Fried), Tomato & Mushroom, 2 Slices Lean Bacon (Cut Off Fat).
    3. 3. Peanut Butter & Honey Rice Cakes: 3-4 Rice Cakes, 30g Peanut Butter, Drizzle of Honey.
    4. 4. Breakfast Wrap: 2 Eggs Scrambled, 20g Cheddar Cheese, 60g Avo Diced, 1 Vegetable or Low Carb Wrap, $1/2$ Tomato Diced, Baby Spinach leaves.
  • Week 4
    1. 1. Jenna Carmen Flapjacks: 3 Jenna Carmen Flapjacks With Honey & 30g Peanut Butter.
    2. 2. Tomato & Cheese Omelette: 3 Eggs, 30g Mozzarella Cheese, 1/4 Tomato Diced, Chopped Onion.
    3. 3. Protein Smoothie: 1 Scoop Jenna Carmen Shake, 1 Cup Ice, 250ml Plant-based or Fat Free Milk & 1tbsp Peanut butter.
    4. 4. Crackerbread Please: 3-4 Crackerbread, 2 Boiled eggs, Lightly Buttered.
  • Week 5
    1. 1. Jenna Carmen Flapjacks: 3 Jenna Carmen Flapjacks, 20g Peanut / Almond Butter, Drizzle With Honey, 5 almonds.

    2. 2. Protein Smoothle: 1 Scoop Jenna Carmen Shake, 1 Cup Ice, 250ml Plant-based or Fat Free Milk & 1Tbsp Peanut butter.

    3. 3. Breakfast Wrap: 2 Eggs Scrambled, 20g Cheddar Cheese, 50g Avocado Diced, 1 Vegetable or Low Carb Wrap, 1/2 Tomato Diced, Baby Spinach leaves.

    4. 4. Yogurt Bowl: 175ml Plain Full Cream Yogurt, Drizzle Honey, 150g Blueberries, 6 Almonds.

  • Week 6
    1. 1. Avocado on Rice Cakes: 2-3 Rice Cakes, Haif a Avocado.

    2. 2. Scrambled Eggs: 3-4 Provitas, 3 Scrambled Eggs.

    3. 3. Blueberry Protein Oats Please: 35g-40g Oats (Weigh Raw), 1 Scoop Jenna Carmen Protein Shake, 100g Blueberries, 5 Almonds.

    4. 4. Jenna Carmen Flapjacks: 3 Jenna Carmen Flapjacks With Drizzle Honey & 1 Tbsp Peanut Butter, 4 Almonds.

  • Week 7
    1. 1. Peanut Butter Crackerbread: 3-4 Crackerbread, 30g Peanut Butter.

    2. 2. Berry & Yogurt Parfait: 150ml Low Fat Yogurt, 150g Fresh Mixed Berries.

    3. 3. Protein Smoothie: 1 Scoop Jenna Carmen Shake, 1 Cup Ice, 250ml Plant-based or Fat Free Milk & 1tbsp Peanut butter, 80g Mixed Berries.

    4. 4. Egg White Omelette Please: 3-4 Egg Whites, Tomato & Onion, 80g Chicken (Weigh Cooked), 60g Avocado.

  • Week 8
    1. 1. Jenna Carmen Flapjacks: 3 Jenna Carmen Flapjacks With Honey & 80g Strawberries/ Blueberries.

    2. 2. Eggs Please: 2 Fried / Poached Eggs, 80g Avocado, Tomatoes, Mushrooms & Onion.

    3. 3. Tomato & Mozzarella Rice Cakes: 2-4 Rice Cakes, 3-4 Slices Tomato, 35g Mozzarella.

    4. 4. Breakfast Wrap: 30g Cheddar Cheese, 60g Avo Diced, 1 Vegetable or Low Carb Wrap, $1/2$ Tomato Diced, Baby Spinach leaves.

    2. 2. Pesto Pasta: 150g Cauliflower Pasta (Weigh Raw), 1 Tbsp Pesto, 20g Fine Parmesan, Handful Chopped Basil.

    3. 3. Beetroot & Chickpea Salad: 100g Cooked Beetroot, 120g Chickpeas (Tinned), 100g Mixed Greens, 30g Feta Cheese, 15ml Lemon Juice.

    1. 1. Chicken & Hummus Bowl: 120g Chicken (Weigh Cooked), 2-3Tbsp Hummus, Cucumber, 75g Cherry Tomatoes, 3-4 Provitas.

    2. 2. Soup please: 300ml Beef & Lentil Soup.

    3. 3. Fish & Green Bowl: 150g White Fish (Weigh Cooked), 150g Cauliflower Rice (Weigh Raw), Mixed Greens, 60g Avocado, Lemon Juice.

    4. 4. Spinach & Feta Stuffed Chicken: 150g Chicken Breast (Weigh Cooked), Spinach, 30g Feta Cheese, Served with 150g Roasted Butternut.

  • Week 6

    3. 3. Chicken Salad Bowl: 150g Chicken (Weigh Cooked), 100g Mixed Greens, 60g Avocado, 1 Boiled Egg, 2-3 Provitas.

    4. 4. Chickpea & Spinach Wrap: 1 Low Carb / Vegetable Wrap, 120g Chickpeas (Tinned), 50g Spinach Leaves, 50g Diced Tomato, 2 Tbsp Fat Free Cottage Cheese.

    2. 2. Egg Salad Bowl: 2 Boiled Eggs, Mixed Greens, 60g Avocado, 2-3 Provitas.

    3. 3. Grilled Steak Salad: 150g Grilled steak (Weigh Cooked), Mixed Greens, 100g Roast Sweet Potato Chunks, 30g Crumbled Feta.

    4. 4. Lentil & Avo Salad: 100g Lentils (Cooked), 100g Avo (Diced), 75g Cherry Tomatoes, 30g Red Onion, Lime Vinaigrette.
